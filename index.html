Our Story

A Taste of Home in Stafford

Gloria Luz Market Y Restaurante brings the authentic flavors of El Salvador and Central America to Stafford, Virginia. What started as a small grocery store serving the local Latin American community has grown into a beloved neighborhood destination.

Our kitchen makes everything fresh, from scratch, every single day. The pupusas are handmade to order. The soups simmer for hours. The carne asada hits the grill when you walk through the door. This is home cooking, not fast food.

Whether you are stopping by for a quick meal or stocking up on imported products from back home, Gloria Luz is your one-stop shop for everything Latin American in Stafford.
